Here is the transcription of the text in the image:

**Mweri Mĩrongo ĩrĩ na mũgwanja, mweri wa Njariarĩ**
**Date 27, Month - January),**

**Njumatatũ** - **Monday**

**Words related to cuisine**

**Kũruga** - to cook

**Iruga** - a lot of cooking; a feast

**Mũrugi** - a cook; Chef

**Arugi** - Cooks, Chefs

**Mwaki** - Fire

**Gũakia mwaki** - to light a fire

**Mwaki wa ngũ** - Wood fire

**Mwaki wa makara** - Charcoal fire

**Mwaki wa thitima** - Electric fire

**Mwaki wa gas** - gas fire (there's no word for cooking gas)

**Riiko rĩa ngũ** - hearth that uses firewood

**Njiko ya makara** - a cooker that uses charcoal

**Nyũngũ** - earthenware pot
