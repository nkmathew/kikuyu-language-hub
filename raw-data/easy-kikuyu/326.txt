Ũyũ nũũ? Who is this?
Ũyũ nĩ mũtumia This is a woman
Akuĩte kĩ? What is she carrying?
akuĩte ngũ She is carrying firewood
Na kĩĩ kĩngĩ? And what else?
Na kana (mwana) and a child
Ni kahĩĩ kana nĩ kairĩtu? Is it a boy or a girl?
ngwĩciria nĩ kahĩĩ I think it is a boy
