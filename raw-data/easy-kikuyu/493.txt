## Transcription: Kikuyu News and Vocabulary

### Wakiunia – Sunday

**Mohoro ma Kiumia Gĩkĩ**
**This Week's News**

**ŨHORO WA KŨHÃHŨRA** – **Shocking news**

* **Kũrerĩkana atĩ mbara ya ungumania nĩ nguũ** - **It is being said that the war on corruption is dead**
* **Ciana cia thukuru cia Ganze, cikaragĩra mahiga cĩgĩthooma.** **School children of Ganze sit on stones as they study (learn)**
* **Cirahũrĩwo mbica cikarĩĩre mahiga** **They were photographed seated on stones**
* **na cithĩũrũrĩkĩrĩrwo nĩ maĩ** **and surrounded by water**
* **thutha wa kabura ka ndagĩka ikũmi na ithano** **After a drizzle that lasted fifteen minutes.**
