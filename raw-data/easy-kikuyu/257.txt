### Key Takeaways
1.  **Singular Prefix:** The 3rd person singular subject prefix is **a-** (from the pronoun *We*).
2.  **Plural Prefix:** The 3rd person plural subject prefix is **ma-** (from the pronoun *O*).
3.  **Progressive Marker:** The progressive marker is **-ra-** which is inserted before the verb stem to denote an ongoing action ("is...ing").
    * Example: **A-ra-roka** (He/She is coming) and **Ma-ra-roka** (They are coming).
4.  **Tone/Vowel Length:** The image notes that the pronouns *We* and *O* have a **short vowel** in this context.
