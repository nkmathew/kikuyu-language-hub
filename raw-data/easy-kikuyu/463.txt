## Rũgano rũkũhĩ - short story

**Translation 3**

**Bowes atumire ndũgũ** -
**Bowes made friends**

**na mũnene wetagwo Wangombe** -
**With a leader known as Wangombe**

**kinya rĩmwe agathĩĩ** -
**Until once, he went**

**mbara kũrũa** - **to war, to fight**

**e mwena wa Wangombe** -
**On Wangombe's side**

**Bowes aikarire na agĩkũyũ** -
**Bowes stayed with the Kikuyu**

**kahinda karaihu,** - **A long time** ....
