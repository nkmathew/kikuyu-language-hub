### Proverb

**Mũndũ Mũgĩ ndarĩ mĩhere ya ũhoro**

**Meaning:** A clever person does not need too much explaining.
*(This proverb is a compliment, suggesting that a wise or intelligent person can grasp an idea quickly without needing elaborate detail.)*

---

### Vocabulary Breakdown

* **Mũgĩ** - Clever
* **Mĩhere** - methods of giving (telling)
* **Mũndũ** - person
* **Ũhoro** - news, information (the matter)
