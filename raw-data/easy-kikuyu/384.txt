## 2. Kikuyu Proverb and Vocabulary (Wakana – Thursday)

| Kikuyu Word/Phrase | English Translation |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Wakana** | Thursday |
| **Thimo** | proverb |

**Mũtĩ ũrũngagwo ũrĩ mũnini**
A tree is straightened when young (when still a sapling)

*(Implied meaning: Do not wait till your kids are adults)*

### Vocabulary
* **Mũtĩ** - tree
* **Kũrũũnga** - to correct, make straight
* **Mũnini** - small, young
* **Taara** - give advice

## 3. Kikuyu Grammar and Proverb

### Forming Plural Commands
To tell more than one person to do something, simply add letter '**i**' to the verb.

| Singular Command | Plural Command | English Meaning |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rĩa** (eat) | **Rĩai** | Eat! (plural) |
| **Ũka** (come) | **Ũkai** | Come! (plural) |
| **Thii** (go) | **Thĩĩi** | Go! (plural) |
| **Koma** (sleep) | **Komai** | Sleep! (plural) |
| **Andĩka** (write) | **Andĩkai** | Write! (plural) |

### Proverb
**Toro ti gĩkuo**
Sleep is not death.
