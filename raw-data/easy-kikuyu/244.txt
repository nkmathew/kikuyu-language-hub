## Image 1: Past Tenses - Early Today

**Past tenses - 1st person, 2nd person, 3 person**

### **Early today**

**1st person, singular and Plural**
Ndĩthambire na maĩ mahoro
Twĩthambire na maĩ mahoro

**2nd person, singular and Plural**
Wĩthambire na maĩ mahoro
Mwĩthambire na maĩ mahoro

**3rd person, singular and Plural**
Ethambire na maĩ mahoro
Methambire na maĩ mahoro

## Image 2: Sounds and Feelings

**Sounds and feelings**
**Ũraigua atĩa? Ndĩraigua...** - What are you feeling? I am feeling...

| Emoji | Kĩkũyũ Word | English Translation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 😩 | **Guoya** | SCARED |
| 😎 | **Gĩkeno** | HAPPY |
| 😞 | **Kĩeha** | SAD |
| 😠 | **Marakara** | ANGRY |
| 😁 | **Ndũhĩo** | EXCITED |
| 😟 | **Kĩmako** | WORRIED |
| 😮 | **Kũhahũka** | SURPRISED |
| 🤪 | **Ũrĩmũ** | SILLY |
| 😫 | **Kwaga kũiganĩra** | FRUSTRATED |

* **Kũringa kanua** - Warakara, waringa kanua
* **Kũbogotha** - mũrwaru nĩ araria? Aca no nĩ arabogotha
* **Gũthĩoma** - Arathioma akĩaria Gĩkũyũ
* **Kũhaya** - Ndiratwara mwana thibitarĩ tondũ nĩ arahaya
* **Gũkorora** - Kaĩ ũrakorora? Tua gĩkorora
* **Gũathĩmũra** - Nyita kanua ũgĩathĩmũra
* **Gũkomba** - to make bad gestures at someone who is facing away; To borrow money
* **Kũigua Kĩnyiria** - to be disgusted
* **Kũnyĩra** - to express disgust for something (eg a kind of food)
* **Kũnunũha** - fast sounds made by someone crying
* **Kũnunũha** - to nose bleed
* **Gwĩthĩkĩra** - to have a sad posture, eg. When mourning

## Image 3: Do this before you do that

**Do this before you do that**
Nyua maĩ ũtanarĩa irio - **drink water before you eat food**
Nyuai maĩ mûtanarĩa irio - **plural (told to several persons)**
Wĩthambe ũtanauma nja - **shower before you go out**
Mwĩthambei mûtanuma nja - **Plural (nja - out)**
Hũra thimũ ũtaneka ũndũ ũngĩ - **make a call before you do something else;** Hũrai thimũ mûtaneka ũndũ ũngĩ - **plural**
Thikĩrĩrĩa ũtanaria - **listen before you speak**
Thikĩrĩrĩai mûtanaria - **Plural**
Gũra ranji ũtanathĩĩ - **buy lunch before you go**
Gũrai ranji mûtanathĩĩ - **Plural**
Thomithia mwana atananeheha - **teach the child before he/grows (up)**
Thomithiai ciana citananeheha - **plural**
Mũtware thibitarĩ atanarwara makĩria - **take him/her to hospital before he/she gets worse (gets sicker)**
