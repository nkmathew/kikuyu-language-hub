Numbers in Kikuyu
The simple numbers in Kikuyu are as follows:
1. Îmwe Mûndû ûmwe One person
2. Igîrî Andû erî
3. Ithathû Andû atatû
4. Inya Andû ana
5. Ithano Andû atano
6. Ithathatû Andû atandatû
7. Mûgwanja Andû mûgwanja
8. Inyanya Andû anana
9. Kenda Andû kenda
10. Ikûmi Andû ikûmi
After the number ten, the
next numbers are stated as
‘ten and one, ten and two’
and so on.
11. Ikûmi na îmwe Andû ikûmi na ûmwe
