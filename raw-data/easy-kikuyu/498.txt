## Transcription: Kikuyu Proverb and Vocabulary

### Proverb

* **Mũhenania arĩ matukũ**
    * **A liar has days (...days are numbered)**

### Vocabulary

* **Maheni** - **lies**
* **Mũhenania** - **a lier**
* **Matukũ** - **Days; Interestingly, Ũtukũ means night**
* **Arĩ** - **(he/she) has**

### Message

* **Keep telling lies and you will be found out soon**
