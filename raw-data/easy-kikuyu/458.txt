**Mweri, ikumi na ithathatũ** | **Date, 16th**
**Mweri wa kenda** | **9 th Month**

## Do this before you do that

**Kũ - nyua**; **kũ - rĩa**; **Gwĩthamba**; **Kuuma**; **kũhũra thimũ**; **gwĩka**; **gũthikĩrĩrĩa**; **gũthomĩthia**; **kũrwara**; **kwaria**

* **Nyua maĩ ũtanarĩa irio** - drink water before you eat food
* **Nyuai maĩ mũtanarĩa irio** - plural (told to several persons)
* **Wĩthambe ũtanauma nja** - shower before you go out
* **Mwĩthambe mũtanauma nja** - Plural (**nja** - out)
* **Hũra thimũ ũtaneka ũndũ ũngĩ** - make a call before you do something else; **Hũrai thimũ mũtaneka ũndũ ũngĩ** - plural
* **Thikĩrĩrĩa ũtanaria** - listen before you speak
* **Thikĩrĩrĩai mũtanaria** - Plural
* **Gũra ranji ũtanathĩĩ** - buy lunch before you go
* **Gũrai ranji mũtanathĩĩ** - Plural
* **Thomĩthia mwana atanāneneha** - teach the child before he/grows (up)
* **Thomĩthiai ciana citanāneneha** - plural
* **Mũtware thibitarĩ atanarwara makĩria** - take him/her to hospital before he/she gets worse (gets sicker)
