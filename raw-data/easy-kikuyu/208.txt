Wash your hands
Wĩthambe moko

| Step | Instruction (English) | Instruction (Kikuyu) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **WET YOUR HANDS** | Wĩtĩrĩrie mai |
| **2** | **APPLY SOAP** | Wĩhake thabuni |
| **3** | **WASH YOUR HANDS FOR 20 SECONDS** | Wĩthambe moko thekondi mirongo îrî |
| **4** | **RINSE** | Wĩkamũre |
| **5** | **DRY WITH A CLEAN TOWEL** | Wĩhuure na ngacibũ ya karatathi |
| **6** | **USE THE PAPER TOWEL TO TURN OFF THE TAP** | Hũthĩra ngacibũ ya karatathi kũhinga mũberethi |
