Oiga kaũndũ - When he said something
makahũra mahĩ -  they clapped
Rĩmwe oigaga ciugo  - sometimes he said words 
citarĩ cia mathekanio - that were not funny
nao magatheka - and they laughed.

Kiugo - a word, a statement (Ciugo - words)
Njeri, uga kaũndũ Njeri - say something
Uga kaũndũ ka mathekania - say something funny
Churchill nĩ mũndũ wa mathekania - Churchill is a comedian
Mũtongoria akinya, hũrai hĩ - when the boss arrives, clap your hands
Theka - laugh
Nĩ tũtheke hamwe - let us laugh together
Gũkinya - to arrive (said with a monotone)
GũkiNYA - To step on (said with rising tone)
