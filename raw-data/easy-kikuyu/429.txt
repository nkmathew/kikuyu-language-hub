Rĩmai gũkumia - May you get the fruits of your labour
Rĩmai - work on your farms
gũkumia - where you will get results (fruits)
This phrase is used as a blessing to anyone regardless of the profession, whether in business or employment. Kikuyus were traditionally farmers, hence the origin of the phrase.
