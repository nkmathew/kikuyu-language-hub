### Proverb
**Gũtema na kanua ti gũtema na rũhĩũ**

**Meaning:** To slander is not like cutting with a knife.

*(This proverb cautions against the destructive power of words and slander (cutting with the mouth), implying that its damage can be far worse or more permanent than a physical cut.)*

### Vocabulary Breakdown

* **Gũtema** - To cut
* **Na kanua** - with the mouth
* **Rũhĩũ** - a medium sized knife
* **Kahĩũ** - small knife
