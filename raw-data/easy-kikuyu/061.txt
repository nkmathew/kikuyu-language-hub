Hîndî ya Mbura na ya riua 2
The rainy and sunny periods
Rûheni rûûkaga mbere ya marurumî - lightening comes before thunder.
Mbura nî ya ringa - the rainy (season) has start- ed (literally 'the rain has beaten)
Nî kûrûaura ? - is it raining? (vowel a+u = o, ... kûroira")
Nî kûrathuthua - it is drizzling
Ime - dew; Matu - clouds; matu ma mbura - rain clouds (the grey ones); Rûkûngû - Dust.
Mweri - moon (also month)
Kîgûû - flash flood (this word means water in Kîkamba)
Thano - very dry season
Mûkûnga mbura - Rainbow
Kwîna kibii - there is fog
