Ya – of (belonging to)
Thua – limp; gũthua – to limp (when injured)
Dongoria (the lead thing- in this case BULL)
Itikinyĩraga – they don’t reach/arrive; Nyeki - grass
gũkinya - to arrive; to step on
When the lead (bull) limps (is injured), they (other cows) do not find the grass/fodder/food
