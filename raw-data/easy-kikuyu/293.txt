**Kairĩtu gaitũ** - **Our (small) girl**

**Mwana wakwa nĩ aronga** - **My child is suckling**

**Mwana witũ nĩ arongithio** - **our child is being suckled**

**Maitũ nĩ arongithia mwana wake** - **(Our) mother is suckling her child**

**Kaana gaka nĩ gakenge** - **This (small) child is an infant**

**Ũyu nĩ mwana wa rũkenge** - **this child is an infant**

**Mwana ũyũ nĩ wa kahiĩ kana kairĩtu?** - **Is this child a boy or a girl?**

**Nĩ kaana ga kairĩtu?** - **Is the baby a (small) girl?**

**Aca, nĩ kana ga kahiĩ** - **No, the child is a (small) boy**

**Say the above sentences in past tense (yesterday)**
