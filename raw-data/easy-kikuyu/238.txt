## Kĩkũyũ Proverb (Thimo) for Monday

| Kĩkũyũ Phrase | English Translation |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Mũrĩa wiki, akuaga e wiki** | **The one who eats alone dies alone** |

### Vocabulary

* **Kũrĩa** - to eat
* **wiki** - alone
* **Mũrĩa** - one who eats
* **Gũkũa** - to die
* **Akuaga** - he/she dies
