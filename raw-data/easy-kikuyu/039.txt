Kikuyu Lesson 1. week 1-3 | First person conjugation of verbs, Singular and plural

1st person Singular
Nĩĩ - Me
Nĩ ndĩroka - I am coming
Nĩ ndĩrarĩra - I am crying
Nĩ ndĩrarĩa - I am eating
Nĩ ndĩrararia - I am speaking
Nĩ ndĩrakena - I am happy
Nĩ ndĩratheka - I am laughing
Nĩ ndĩrathĩĩ - I am going
Nĩ ndĩrathooma - I am reading
Nĩ ndĩrandĩka - I am writing

1st person Plural
Ithuĩ - We
Nĩ tũroka - We are coming
Nĩ tũrarĩra - We are crying
Nĩ tũrarĩa - We are eating
Nĩ tũrararia - We are speaking
Nĩ tũrakena - We are happy
Nĩ tũratheka - We are laughing
Nĩ tũrathĩĩ - We are going
Nĩ tũrathooma - We are reading
Nĩ tũrandĩka - We are writing
