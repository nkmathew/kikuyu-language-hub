The two images provide vocabulary lessons on **reptiles/amphibians** and **parts of the body** in Kikuyu.

## Nouns: Reptiles and Amphibians

This vocabulary is taken from the image featuring the animals:

| English Name | Kikuyu Name |
|:---|:---|
| Chameleon | **Kĩmbu** |
| Skink/Lizard | **Njagathi** |
| Snake (Cobra shown) | **Nyoka** |
| Crocodile | **Kĩng'ang'i** |
| Frog | **Kĩũra** |
