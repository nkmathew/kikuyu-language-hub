## 1. Grammar: Future Tenses (Second Person Singular)

The images illustrate the **Second Person Singular** (you) future tense, which has different prefixes depending on the time frame.

### ⏳ A Few Moments Later or Anytime Today
The prefixes used for actions happening soon are **Ûgû-, Ûgwî-, Ûkwî-,** and **Ûkû-**.

| Example | Translation | Prefix |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ûgûkîra** dagîka ikûmi ciathira. | You will wake up after ten minutes. | Ûgû- |
| **Ûgwîthamba** na maî mahoro. | You will take a bath with cold water. | Ûgwî- |
| **Ûkwîhumba** nguo naihenya. | You will put on your clothes quickly. | Ûkwî- |

### 🗓️ Anytime Tomorrow and Into the Future
The prefixes used for actions happening tomorrow or beyond are **Ûgo-, Ûge-, Ûke-,** and **Ûga-**.

| Example | Translation | Prefix |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Ûgokîra** dagîka ikûmi ciathira. | You will wake up ten after ten minutes. | Ûgo- |
| **Ûgethamba** na maî mahoro. | You will take a bath with cold water. | Ûge- |
| **Ûkehumba** nguo naihenya. | You will put on your clothes quickly. | Ûke- |
| **Ûkaruga** caî. | You will make tea. | Ûka- |

## 2. Vocabulary: Types of Houses (*Mîthemba ya Nyûmba*)

The lessons cover three main types of traditional and modern houses, focusing on construction materials.

| Type of House | Kikuyu Term | Construction Material | Roofing Material | Other Feature |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stone House** | **Nyûmba ya Mahiga** | **Mahiga** (stones) | **Mabati** (iron sheets) | **Rûthingo rwa mahiga** (stone wall) |
| **Timber House** | **Nyûmba ya mbaû** | **Mbaû** (timber/wood) | **Maturubarĩ** (tiles) | **Rûthingo rwa mbaû** (wooden wall) |
| **Mud Hut** | **Nyûmba ya ndooro** | **Ndooro** (mud) | **Nyeki** (grass) or **ithanjĩ** (reeds) | **Rûthingo rwa ndooro** (mud wall) |
