Here is the transcription of the text in the image:

**Njuma** - **Saturday**

**Mawira** - **Jobs**

**(Nĩĩ) Ndĩ bundi** - I am a handyman (carpenter, plumber etc.)

**(Nĩĩ) Ndĩ bundi wa mbao** - I am a carpenter

**Mbao** - wood, timber; **Cuuma** - metal

**(Wee) wĩ mũrĩmi** - You are a farmer

**(We) nĩ ndereva** - he/she is a driver

**(Inyuĩ) mwĩ atongoria** - You (plural) are leaders

**(O) nĩ borithi** - they are police (men/women)

**Ũyũ nĩ mũbĩa** - this is a priest (catholic)

**The words in brackets can be ignored without change in meaning**
