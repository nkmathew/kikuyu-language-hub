Gũtũnda - to have a rash, 
Ndĩ mũtũndu guoko - I have a rash on my arm,
Thundo - pimple(s), Ndĩna thundo ũthiũ - I have a pimple on my face, 
Kĩronda - a wound, 
E na kironda iru - he/she has a wound on his knee. 
Kũrwara - to be sick. 
Ndĩ mũrwaru nda - I am sick in the stomach
Gũturwo - to have pain
