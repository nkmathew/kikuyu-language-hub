## Kikuyu Proverb and Vocabulary

### Proverb
**Ngai ateithagia wĩteithĩtie**

**Meaning:** God helps he/she who has helped self.
*(This proverb is similar to the English saying, "God helps those who help themselves," emphasizing the importance of effort and initiative.)*

### Vocabulary Breakdown

* **Ngai** - God
* **Teithia** - help
* **Wĩteithie** - help yourself (command)
* **Wĩteithĩtie** - who has helped him/herself
* **Mũteithie** - help him/her (command)
