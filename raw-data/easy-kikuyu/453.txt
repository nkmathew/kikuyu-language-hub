## Words related to sleep

* **Gũcũnga** - to doze
* **Gũkoma** - to sleep
* **Gũkoma na nda** - to sleep on the stom-ach (face down)
* **Gũkoma na ngara** (... na mugongo) - to sleep on your back
* **Gũkoma na mwena** - to sleep on side (eg. On left shoulder)
* **Kũrara** - to spend the night
* **Hinga maitho ũkome** - Shut your eyes and sleep
* **Kaĩ ũkomaga ũhingũrĩte maitho?** - do you sleep with eyes open?
* **Rara nĩ gwatuka** - sleep, darkness has fallen
* **Rarĩrĩra ũgathie rũciũ** - sleep over and go tomorrow
* **Koma ũkĩre thaa igĩrĩ cia rũciũnĩ** - sleep and wake up at 8 in the morning
* **ũkĩra** - wake up
* **ũkĩra nĩ gwakĩa** - Wake up, it is sunrise
