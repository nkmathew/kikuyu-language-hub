Simple Sentences | t, th, ny

t (t) tene
O Na Tene na tene - forever and ever
Tinia taama maita matano - cut the fabric five times
Tara mbeca - count the money, tooria - accomplish, teta akũhe irio - complain so he/she gives you food, tunga karwĩmbo - compose a song

th (th) thaani
Thaani ĩno nĩ theru - this plate is clean
Thooma ũtarĩ na itherũ - read without joking
Thenga haha (ehera haha) - move away from here, thutha wa nyumba - behind the house, thooma ibuku rĩaku - read your book

ny (ny) Nyanya
Ruga Nyanya na nyeni - cook tomatoes and greens
Mũrarũ nĩ Nyoka - Mũrarũ is a snake
Nyamũ cia gĩthaka - wild animals, nyeni nĩ njega - vegetables are good, Nyenje ĩ gĩko - a cockroach is dirty
