**Jũmatatũ** | **Monday**

**Mwaka wa ngiri igĩrĩ na ikũmi na kenda** - the year **2019**

**Ngwĩhoka nĩ mũrarũgire mwaka wega**
**I hope you jumped (crossed over) the year well**

* **Ngwĩhoka** - I hope; **Mwĩhoko** - hope
* **nĩ mũrarũgire** - You jumped (plural)
* **nĩ ũrarũgire** - You jumped (singular)
* **nĩ ndĩrarũgire** - I jumped
* **mwaka** - year
* **Wega** - good

**Ĩĩ, kwa hinya wa ngai** - yes with the power of God

**Mwaka ũyũ nĩtwarie gĩkũyũ mũno**
**Year - this - let's speak - Kikuyu - much**
