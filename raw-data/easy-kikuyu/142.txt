Nīwahingūra -Have you opened?
Ndinahingūra-I have not opened
Nīngūhingūrīte kabere -I opened earlier
Nī wethamba?- Have you taken bath?
Ndinethamba-I have not taken a bath
Nīngwīthambīte kabere-I took a bath earlier.
Nīūrugire cai? -Have you made tea?
Ndinaruga cai.-I have not made tea.
Nīndugire cai hau kabere-I made tea earlier .
