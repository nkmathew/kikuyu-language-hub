## Transcription

**Wagatano - Friday**
**Verb - Enter**

**Kũingĩra - to enter**

**Ingĩra - Enter (said to one per son)**

**Ingĩrai - Enter (said to several persons)**

**Ingĩria - Take/put something in**

**Maingĩrie thĩinĩ wa nyũmba - Take them into the house; thĩinĩ - inside**

**Ingĩria cabi kuburiinĩ - put the key in the padlock**

**Cabi - key; Kuburi - padlock**
