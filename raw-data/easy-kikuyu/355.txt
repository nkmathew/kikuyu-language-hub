### Proverb
**Wĩĩgĩire ndahũũtaga**

**Meaning:** He/she that has saved does not starve.
*(This proverb emphasizes the importance of saving, preparation, and prudence for future security.)*

### Vocabulary Breakdown

* **Kũĩga** - to keep, store
* **Ng'aragu** - Hunger
* **Kũhũũta** - to be hungry
* **Hũũro** - stomach area in animal
    * *eg.* Cow that gets depressed when the animal is hungry.
