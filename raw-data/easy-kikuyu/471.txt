## Verb - Ciara

* **Gũciara** - to give birth
* **Mũciari** - a parent (one who gives birth)
* **Nĩ aciara** - she has given birth
* **Nĩ aciarire** - she gave birth (early today)
* **Nĩ araciarire** - she gave birth (yesterday)
* **Nĩ aciarire** - She gave birth (long ago)
