**Mweri, mũgwanja** | **Date, 7 th**
**Mweri wa kenda** | **9 th Month**

## Words related to food and drink

* **Kũnyua** - to drink; **Kĩndũ gĩa kũnyua** - something to drink
* **Kũrĩa** - to eat; **Kĩndũ gĩa kũrĩa** - something to eat
* **Gũcama** - to taste; **Irio** - food
* **Cama irio ici** - taste this food
* **Cĩĩna cumbĩ?** - does it have salt
* **Ĩĩ nĩ njamũ cumbĩ (ĩĩ nĩ iracama cumbĩ)** - yes it tastes salt
* **Mũrĩo** - sweet; **Kũgagata** - to be sour;
* **Kũrũra** - to be bitter; **To have too much salt, sugar etc.**
* **Irio nĩ ndũrũ cumbĩ** - the food has too much salt
* **Iria igagatũ** - sour milk; **ũcũrũ** - porridge; **Njohi** - beer
* **Mbembe cĩĩ na mũrĩo?** - Is the maize sweet?
* **Irio cia gũtherũkio** - boiled food
* **Irio cia gũkarangwo** - fried food
* **Kũhũta** - to be hungry; **Wĩ mũhũtu** - are you hungry?
* **Aca, ndĩ mũhũnu** - I am full (satisfied)
* **Nyama ya kũhĩhĩo** - roasted meat; **mbembe ya** ..........
