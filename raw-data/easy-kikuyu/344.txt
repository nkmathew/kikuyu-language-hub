## Thimo ya Ũmũthĩ (Proverb for Today)

**Kahora karĩ indo**

**Meaning:** Patience (steadiness) has (brings) wealth.

### Vocabulary Breakdown

* **Kahora** – slow, steady
* **Indo** – things, wealth
* **Kĩndũ** – something

The proverb essentially means that taking things slowly and maintaining a steady effort will eventually lead to prosperity.
