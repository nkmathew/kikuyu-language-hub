PROVERB OF THE WEEK
Njogu ndĩremagwo nĩ mũguongo wayo – the elephant is not burdened by its ivory
Njogu – Elephant (also a man’s name)

Kũremwo – to be unable
Nĩndaremwo… – I have been unable…
Ndiremagwo… – I am never unable…
Nĩnemagwo… – I am unable (continuously)…
Nyamũ ĩno nĩ njogu - this animal is an elephant

Mũguongo – Ivory
Mũguongo Wayo – its ivory
Mũti Wakwa – my stick (tree)
