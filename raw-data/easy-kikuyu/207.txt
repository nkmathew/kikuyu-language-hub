Words related to sleep

**Gũcũnga** - to doze
**Gũkoma** - to sleep
**Gũkoma na nda** - to sleep on the stomach (face down)
**Gũkoma na ngara** (... na mũgongo) - to sleep on your back
**Gũkoma na mwena** - to sleep on one side
**Kũrara** - to spend the night
**Hinga maitho ũkome** - Shut your eyes and sleep
**Kaĩ ũkomaga ũhingũrĩte maitho?** - do you sleep with eyes open?
**Rara nĩ gwatuka** - sleep-over, darkness has fallen
**Rara ũgathie rĩciũ** - sleep over and go tomorrow
**Koma ũkĩre thaa igĩrĩ cia rũciũ-inĩ** - sleep and wake up at 8 in the morning
**Koma na wega** (thayũ) - Sleep well
**Gĩa na ũtuko mwega** - have a good night
**Korwo na ũtuko mwega** - have a good night
