## Transcription: Kikuyu Clothing Vocabulary

### Header

* **Mweri ithano, mweri wa ĩnana**
* **Date 5th, Month - eighth (August)**
* **Njumatatũ** - **Monday**

### Words related to clothing

* **Thuruaĩ ngũhĩ** - **Shorts**
* **Thuruaĩ ndaihu** - **Trousers**
* **Mũthurũ (thigatĩ)** - **Skirt**
* **Caatĩ (nguo ya ngoro)** - **Shirt**
* **Mburãũthĩ, (nguo ya ngoro)** - **Blouse**
* **Nguo ya thĩinĩ** - **Underwear**
* **Gĩtambaya kĩa mũtwe** - **Head scarf**
* **Gĩtambaya gĩa kĩmira** - **handkerchief**
* **Thogithĩ** - **Socks**
* **Kĩratũ** - **shoe (pair of shoes)**
* **Kĩratũ gĩa kobo** - **Gum boots**
* **Kĩratũ gĩa taritari** - **Slippers**
* **Kĩratũ kĩa mũtheece** - **High heels (shoe)**
