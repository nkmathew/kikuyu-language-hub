The image provides a vocabulary list for the major organs of the human **digestive system** in Kikuyu.

The title, **Ciĩga cia njĩra ya irio**, translates to:
* **Ciĩga** means **parts** or **organs**.
* **cia** means **of the**.
* **njĩra** means **way** or **path**.
* **ya** means **of**.
* **irio** means **food**.
* So, **Ciĩga cia njĩra ya irio** literally means **"The parts of the path of food"** or **"The digestive system."**
