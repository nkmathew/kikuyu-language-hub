## Kikuyu Phrases for Understanding

These phrases are used to ask if someone has understood:

* **Nĩ waigua?**
    * Do you hear (understand)?

* **Nĩ wanyita?**
    * Do you catch (understand)?

* **Nĩ twaiguana?**
    * Do we understand each other?

### Related Vocabulary

* **Igua** - hear;
* **Igua na matũ merĩ**
    * Hear (listen) with both ears (A phrase meaning to listen very attentively or seriously).
