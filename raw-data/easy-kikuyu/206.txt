Simple Sentences t, th, ny

T (t) tene
t (t) teneO na Tene na tene - forever and ever
Tinia taama maita matano - cut the fabric five times
Tara mbeca - count the money
tooria - accomplish
teta akũhe irio - complain so he/she gives you food
tunga karwĩmbo - compose a song

Ũ (th) thaani
Ũ (th) thaaniThaani ĩno nĩ theru - this plate is clean
Thooma ũtarĩ na itherũ - read without joking
Thenga haha (ehera haha) - move away from here
thutha wa nyumba - behind the house
thooma ibuku rĩaku - read your book

I) (ny) Nyanya
I) (ny) NyanyaRuga Nyanya na nyeni - cook tomatoes and greens
Mũrarũ nĩ Nyoka - Mũrarũ is a snake
Nyamũ cia gĩthaka - wild animals
nyeni nĩ njega - vegetables are good
Nyenje ĩ gĩko - a cockroach is dirty
