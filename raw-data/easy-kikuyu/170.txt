## 2. Health and Hospital Phrases

This section provides vocabulary and common phrases related to illness and visiting a hospital (**Thibitarĩ**).

### Vocabulary

| Kikuyu Term | English Meaning | Image Context |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Thibitarĩ** | Hospital | Building exterior |
| **Mũrwaru** | Patient / Sick person | Person on a hospital bed |
| **Ndagĩtarĩ** | Doctor (male) | Man in a lab coat |
| **Mũrigitani** | Doctor (female/general term) | Woman in a lab coat |
| **Dagĩtarĩ wa magego** | Dentist | Person sitting in a dental chair |
| **Mĩthaiga** | Medicine / Drugs | Pills and bottles |
| **Ndawa** | Medicine / Remedy | Pills and bottles |

### Dialogue and Phrases

| Kikuyu Phrase | English Translation |
|:---|:---|
| **Ndĩ mũrwaru** | I am sick |
| **Ndiraiguia wega** | I am not feeling well |
| **Thĩĩ thibitarĩ** | Go to the hospital |
| **Ũraatũũrwo nĩ kũ?** | Where are you hurting (where is your pain)? |
| **Ndiraatũũrwo nĩ mũtwee** | I have a headache |
| **Nĩ ũraatũũrwo nĩ nda?** | Do you have a stomachache? |
| **Aca ndiraatũũrwo nĩ nda** | No I do not have a stomachache |
| **Ũraatũũrwo nĩ kũũ ũngĩ?** | Where else are you hurting? |
| **O na igego nĩ rĩraatũũra** | I also have a toothache |
| **Thĩĩ wone ndagĩtarĩ wa magego** | Go see a dentist |
| **Eha mũrigitanĩ** | Where is the nurse? |
| **Amba ũthĩmwo ratirĩ** | Get your weight checked first |
| **Hĩĩ! Wĩ na ũrugarĩ!** | Wah! Your temperature is high |
