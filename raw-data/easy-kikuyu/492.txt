## Transcription: Kikuyu Vocabulary and Phrases

### Juma – Saturday

**Wĩra wakwa - My job**

* **(Nĩĩ) Ndĩ ndereba wa rorĩ** - I am a **lorry driver**
* **Ndwairithagia rorĩ kuma Nairobi kinya Mombasa** - I drive a lorry from **Nairobi to Mombasa**
* **(Wee) wĩ mũndũ wa kũndeithia** - You are a **person to help me (my helper)**
* **Ũtwarithagia kuma mombatha kinya Nairobi** - You **drive from Mombasa to Nairobi**
* **Twendagia mboga** - We **sell cabbages**
* **(Inyũĩ) mwĩ agũrĩ a mboga** - You are **buyers of cabbages**
* **(O) nĩ aicĩ** - they are **thieves**

* **Nĩ emenyerere** - **He/she should take care of themselves**
