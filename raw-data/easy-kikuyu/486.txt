## Transcription: Kikuyu Phrases about Food and Cooking

### Jumatatũ – Monday

| Kikuyu Phrase | English Translation |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Tũhe ndeto** | Give us information |
| **Ũrĩaga kĩĩ rũũcinĩ?** | What do you eat in the morning? |
| **Ũrĩaga kĩĩ raanjĩ?** | What do you eat at lunchtime? |
| **Ĩ whaĩnĩ?** | What about in the evening? |
| **Nĩ kĩĩ ũũĩ kũruga wega?** | What do you know how to cook well? |
| **Nĩ ũũĩ kũruga ngima?** | Do you know how to cook ugali? |
| **Maitũgwo endete kũruga kĩĩ?** | What does your mother like to cook? |
| **Thogwo amenete kũrĩa kĩĩ?** | What does your father hate to eat? |
| **Wee nĩ kĩĩ wendete kũrĩa mũno?** | What do you love eating most? |
