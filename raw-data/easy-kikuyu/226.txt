## Exercise: Compose Sentences (3rd Person Future Tense - 'Early Today')

The exercise asks you to pick any 10 words and their opposites (5 singular pairs and 5 plural pairs) and compose sentences using the 3rd Person Future Tense (singular and plural), which utilizes the **Nĩ e-** (singular) and **Nĩ me-** (plural) prefixes for the 'later today' future tense.

**Example Conjugations:**
* He/She **will help** (later today): Nĩ **egũteithia** (from *gũteithia* - to help)
* They **will help** (later today): Nĩ **megũteithia**
* He/She **will plant** (later today): Nĩ **ekũhanda** (from *kũhanda* - to plant)
* They **will plant** (later today): Nĩ **mekũhanda**

**Example (3rd P Plural) from the image:** **Mahurunjei mbembe, ndamera monganie ciothe** (This sentence structure is not a correct example for the future tense of the word list provided, as it uses the *Subjunctive* mood, not the *Future Tense* as requested by the exercise instructions.)

### **1. Singular Pairs (5 sentences)**

| Verb Pair | Singular Future Sentence | English Translation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Gũgetha** / **Kũhanda** | Nĩ **ekũgetha** mahũa ciana ciatananĩrĩra. | He/She **will harvest** the flowers before the children arrive. |
| **Kũrĩra** / **Gũtheka** | Nĩ **egũtheka** nĩ ũndũ wa wĩra wake mũga. | He/She **will laugh** because of his/her good work. |
| **Gũtonga** / **Gũthĩna** | Mũthuri ũcio nĩ **egũthĩna** tondũ ndarutaga wĩra. | That man **will be poor** because he doesn't work. |
| **Gũthĩnia** / **Gũteithia** | Nĩ **egũteithia** mami wĩra-inĩ wa mũgũnda. | He/She **will help** mum with the farm work. |
| **Gũtaara** / **Kũhĩtithia** | Nĩ **ekũhĩtithia** mũrata wake arĩ njĩra. | He/She **will mislead** his/her friend on the way. |

### **2. Plural Pairs (5 sentences)**

| Verb Pair | Plural Future Sentence | English Translation |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
