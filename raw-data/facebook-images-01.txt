**PROVERB OF THE WEEK**

**Ũbataire ndaconokaga** – the person in need is not ashamed (to ask for help)

**Kũbatara** – to need
**Bata** – need (pronounced as **BHATA**)
**Nĩ ũrabatara** ? Do you need (Nĩ ũbatare?)
**Ũbataire** – short for ; **Mũndũ ũbataire** – a person in need
**Gũconoka** – to be ashamed
**Gũconora** – to shame (someone)
**Kaĩ ũtangĩconoka** ? – are you not ashamed (**Kaĩ** – one of those words that are not used alone. It implies pity or regret when placed before a sentence – **Kaĩ nĩndamwĩrire-ĩ** – I told him (implying that now something bad has happened )
**Ta conoka** – get ashamed
