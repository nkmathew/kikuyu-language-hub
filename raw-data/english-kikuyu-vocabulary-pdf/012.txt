## Kikuyu Proverb and Translation

| Kikuyu Proverb | English Translation |
|:---|:---|
| **Wĩĩgĩire ndahũtaga** | **He/she that has saved something does not starve** |

---

## Vocabulary

| Kikuyu Word | English Meaning |
|:---|:---|
| **Kũĩga** | to keep, store |
| **Ng'aragu** | Hunger |
| **Kũhũta** | to be hungry |

## Lesson

The image concludes with the practical advice derived from the proverb:

**Make a habit of saving for hard times**
