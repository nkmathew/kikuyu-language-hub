Kikuyu cha kuomba chakula
1. Mūrīega?
2. Tūrīega mūno no anga wee - we are fine, maybe you
3. O na niī ndī mwega - am also fine
        .... No ng'aragu - just famine
4. Gūkū kūrī na mūkimo na gītoero kīa nyama - here we have mūkimo and meat stew
5. Ndīmūhūtu mūno - I am very had hungry
6. Wīthambe moko ngūhe tūirio - wash your hands I give you some food
7. Ūrathimwo mūno, nīndahūna - be very blessed, I am full/satisfied
Note:
Nga'ragu - famine
Ndī na ng'aragu - I am famished
Ndī mūhūtu - I am very hungry
Kūng'ara - to dry up
Ndī mūng'aru - I am dried up / thirsty
Ndī mūnyotu- I am thirsty
