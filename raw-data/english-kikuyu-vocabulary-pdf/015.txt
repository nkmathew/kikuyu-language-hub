Kikuyu cha kuomba maji
1. Wī mwega ? 
2. Ndīmwega mūno
3. He maaī ma kūnyua - Give me water to drink
4. Gūkū maaī ti matherūkie - here, water has not been boiled
5. Ndī mūnyotu mūno- I am very thirsty
6. Gūra maī ma cuba- buy bottled water
7. Ndirī na mbeca - I don't have money
8. Ke maaī ma mūberethi - take (this) tap water
9. Nī ngatho, nī ndanyotoka - thanks I am now de-thirsted (satisfied)
Ofcourse English has no 'de-thirsted.'
